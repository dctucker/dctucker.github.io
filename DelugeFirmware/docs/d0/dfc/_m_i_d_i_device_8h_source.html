103 // These are stored as full-range 16-bit values (scaled up from 7 or 14-bit MIDI depending on which), and you'll want to scale this up again to 32-bit to use them.
104 // X and Y may be both positive and negative, and Z may only be positive (so has been scaled up less from incoming bits).
